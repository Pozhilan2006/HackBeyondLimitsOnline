About the Hackathon

Welcome to Hack Beyond Limits: Online Odyssey, a groundbreaking 48-hour global hackathon that transcends traditional boundaries of innovation and collaboration.

Whether you're a coding wizard, a design genius, or a visionary thinker, this is your opportunity to join forces with like-minded innovators and build solutions that could shape our digital future.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate in the hackathon?

Anyone with a passion for technology can participate! Whether you're a student, professional, or enthusiast, as long as you're 18+ years old, you're welcome to join. Teams can consist of 2-4 members.

Is there a registration fee?

No, participation in Hack Beyond Limits is completely free! We believe in making innovation accessible to everyone.

What if I don't have a team?

No worries! We'll have a team formation session at the beginning of the event where you can meet other participants and form teams. You can also use our Discord channel to connect with potential teammates before the event.

What should I bring to the hackathon?

Since this is an online event, you'll need a computer with a stable internet connection, any software you plan to use, and your creativity! Make sure you have a comfortable setup as you'll be coding for 48 hours.

Can I start working on my project before the hackathon?

No, all projects must be started and developed during the 48-hour hackathon period. You can brainstorm ideas beforehand, but no coding or design work should be done until the official start.

How will projects be judged?

Projects will be evaluated based on innovation, technical complexity, practical implementation, user experience, and presentation. Our panel of judges includes industry experts from the AI and Web3 domains.
